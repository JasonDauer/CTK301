My YouTube Channel

I started my channel 3 years ago and I used to upload videos from making clips off of a website called Renderforest. Renderforest is a website where you can create your own videos, thumbnails, websites, etc. My preference in Rendforest is using the videos option. When you click on videos, you'll have many options to choose which type of video you want to create. I'm a huge fan of creating videos with 3D. I made my YouTube profile pic and background on a website called Canva. Canva is a website where you can make anything like pictures, icons, slides, it's pretty similar to Photoshop in a way.
